Hello my name is Kylie Tsering.

Throughout the years I have intuitively pursued my interests in art, business, design, culture, language, and philosophy. Expansion and exploration have been a common thread from working as a textile designer, to studying logic systems through Tibetan debate in a traditional monastic setting. Working as an editorial stylist and having completed a mentorship program focusing on UX design and front end development have furthered my diverse background. My pursuits have lead me to different areas of the world and have evolved my perspectives and strengthened my creative drive. I enjoy connecting all of my influences to create user-oriented, effective designs.
