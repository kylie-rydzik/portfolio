Hello, my name is Kylie Tsering.

Throughout the years I have intuitively pursued my interests in design, art, culture, language, and philosophy. Expansion and exploration have been common threads throughout my experiences. Completing a mentorship program focusing on UX design and front end development has furthered my diverse background.

After living in New York City for ten years, I decided to leave my job as a textile designer to focus on self-awareness and to learn the Tibetan language so I could study Buddhism in northern India in an authentic manner. Now back in the USA, I am drawn to UX design because it allows me to combine creativity and psychology. I love to be in an industry where ideas drive design. I believe in doing unexpected and extraordinary things. I welcome challenges and thrive in dynamic environments.
